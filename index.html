FAQ

Common Questions

Who is this playbook for?
Amazon sellers who want to add TikTok Shop, TikTok Shop sellers who want Amazon stability, and new cross-border entrepreneurs who want to build on both platforms from day one.
Do I need to already sell on Amazon or TikTok to use this?
The playbook is designed for sellers with some cross-border experience. It includes setup guides for both platforms, but the core value is in the dual-platform integration strategy.
How is this different from the TikTok Shop Playbook?
The TikTok Shop Playbook covers TikTok Shop only. This playbook covers Amazon and TikTok together — how to integrate supply chain, content, and advertising across both platforms.
Does this cover USA, UK, and other markets?
Yes. The playbook covers Amazon.com and TikTok Shop US as the primary markets, with applicable strategies for Amazon EU, UK, and TikTok Shop UK/SEA markets.
What format is the playbook in?
Instant PDF download. 70+ pages, 12 chapters plus appendices with tool comparisons, compliance checklists, and a reusable product scorecard template.
Can I use the same product for both Amazon and TikTok Shop?
Yes — that's the core of this playbook. You source once and sell on both platforms using different SKUs. The playbook covers packaging strategy, inventory allocation, and supplier negotiation for dual-platform selling.
How do I drive traffic from TikTok to my Amazon listing?
The playbook covers 4 methods: link-in-bio tools like Beacons.ai, keyword redirect (guide users to search your brand on Amazon), QR codes in TikTok Shop packaging, and creator affiliate links with Amazon Attribution tracking.
What budget do I need to start selling on both platforms?
The playbook includes a detailed budget allocation model by lifecycle stage. For launch phase (month 1), it recommends approximately $2,000/month total with 70% on TikTok Spark Ads and creator seeding, and 30% on Amazon PPC exact match keywords.
Should I use FBA or FBT for TikTok Shop fulfillment?
The playbook recommends a phased approach: start with self-fulfillment for TikTok, move to FBT (Fulfilled by TikTok) at month 3-6, and maintain FBA for Amazon with a 3PL buffer. Full shipping cost comparison and inventory rebalancing strategies included.
How is dual-platform selling different from single-platform?
Dual-platform sellers report 2-3x faster inventory turnover, 40% lower blended customer acquisition cost, and higher first-year survival rates. Amazon provides stable search traffic while TikTok provides viral discovery traffic.
What product categories work best for Amazon and TikTok together?
The best dual-platform categories are phone accessories, home organization, beauty tools, kitchen gadgets, toys, and fitness accessories. The playbook includes a Product Quadrant Framework to evaluate any product's fit.
